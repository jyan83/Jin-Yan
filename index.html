About (CV)

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Civil, Construction and Environment Engineering at Iowa State University. My adviser is Dr. Simon Laflamme and Dr. An Chen. My research focuses on Structural Health Monitoring. I received an MS in Civil and Environmental Engineering from the Georgia Institute of Technology in 2016, and a Bachelors of Civil Engineering from the Central South University in 2015.
